Welcome to Sahasya

Your journey to better mental health starts here. At Sahasya, we understand that life can be overwhelming, and sometimes, you just need someone to listen.

This platform is dedicated to supporting you with tools for self-discovery and mental wellness. Explore our quiz to understand your mental state better or chat with our friendly, supportive bot when you need someone to talk to.

Our Mission and Features

Non-Judgmental Support

Feel free to express yourself. Our chatbot is here to listen and offer supportive responses without any judgment.

Personalized Quiz

Take our specially designed quiz to gain insights into your mental well-being and get personalized feedback.

Accessible Anytime, Anywhere

Access Sahasya anytime you need support, whether you're on your phone or computer. We’re here for you, day or night.

Confidential & Private

Your privacy is our priority. Conversations and quiz results are kept strictly confidential and secure.

Meet the Team

Sahasya was created by a team of passionate developers studying in VIT Chennai to help people feel better. We are committed to creating a positive, supportive environment and to helping people on their journey to mental wellness.

Our Mission: To provide a safe and supportive environment for everyone seeking mental health support, judgment-free.
